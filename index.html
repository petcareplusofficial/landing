Overview

PetCare+ is a smart, all-in-one platform designed to simplify pet care for owners. Unlike traditional apps that only offer reminders, PetCare+ brings together health tracking, allergy management, diet and supplement planning, appointments, breed-specific guidance, and AI chat support in one place. With clear reports and analytics, pet owners can easily monitor their pet’s well-being and make informed decisions anytime, from desktop or mobile.

Our Features

Pet Health

The Pet Health feature helps you keep all your pet’s medical information organized and accessible. From vaccination and medication records to vet visits and growth tracking, everything is stored in one place.

Smart Ai insights

Smart AI Insights gives pet owners instant answers and guidance without the wait. Whether it’s questions about diet, activity, allergies, or general care, the AI chat support provides reliable suggestions tailored to your pet’s needs.

Report based

The Reports & Analytics feature turns your pet’s data into easy-to-understand insights. From health records and diet patterns to activity trends and appointment history, everything is presented through clear visuals and charts.

Appointments and Reports

Users can quickly schedule vet appointments and easily cancel them when needed. Clear status indicators and reminders help keep pet care on track.
